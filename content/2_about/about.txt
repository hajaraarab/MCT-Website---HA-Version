Courseinfotitle: What is this Course About ?

----

Courseinfotext:

- Discover the many facets of multimedia: websites & apps, 3D & XR, audio & video.
- Become an expert in high-end technology and a creator of media productions and useful tools — from livestreams to the metaverse.
- Multimedia is a team sport for problem solvers: collaborate on projects, think logically, and innovate creatively.
- Experiment in our FabLab with professional cameras, microphones, VR headsets, Arduinos, Oculus Rifts, Kinects, 3D printers, laser cutters, and of course monitors and mobile devices.  The first year is the same for everyone; after that, you can personalize your study program by choosing between the Web & Mobile, 3D & XR, or Audio/Video profiles.  During coaching sessions and project weeks, you’ll discover your talents and which part of the sector you want to work in.  Media or production house, advertising or event agency, startup or multinational — whatever your interest, there’s a strong chance you’ll land a job immediately after your internship.  International vibes? Study in English and meet students from all over the world in Multimedia & Creative Technologies.
